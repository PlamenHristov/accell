01 — About Us

Who we are

Accell is a leading robotics research company dedicated to advancing the field of autonomous systems, machine learning, and human-robot interaction.

Founded by a team of researchers and engineers from top institutions, we combine academic rigor with practical innovation to solve real-world challenges.

Our multidisciplinary team works at the intersection of mechanical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence to create robots that can perceive, reason, and act in complex environments.

02 — Research Areas

What we work on

⬡

Autonomous Navigation

Developing robust perception and planning algorithms for robots to navigate complex, dynamic environments without human intervention.

◈

Machine Learning

Applying deep learning and reinforcement learning to enable robots to learn from experience and adapt to new situations.

⟡

Human-Robot Interaction

Creating intuitive interfaces and collaborative systems that allow humans and robots to work together safely and effectively.

⚙

Mechatronics

Designing and building novel robotic hardware with advanced actuation, sensing, and control systems.

◉

Computer Vision

Developing visual perception systems that enable robots to understand and interpret their surroundings in real-time.

⬢

Multi-Robot Systems

Researching coordination and communication protocols for swarms of robots working together on complex tasks.
